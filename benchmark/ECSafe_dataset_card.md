# ECSafe: Everyday Child Safety Formalisation Benchmark

## Dataset Summary
ECSafe is a benchmark of ~80 core domestic child-safety scenarios designed to evaluate the formal expressiveness, semantic faithfulness, and verification value of LLM-mediated formalisation pipelines.

## Intended Use
The dataset is intended for evaluating systems that translate natural-language safety descriptions into formal transition systems. It is **not** intended for training automated safety certification systems for real-world deployment.

## Scenario Categories
1. Stairs and falls
2. Windows and balconies
3. Pools and water
4. Medicine and hazardous-substance access
5. Burns and hot surfaces
6. Furniture and climbing
7. Road and driveway access
8. Electrical or mixed hazards

## Limitations
The scenarios are author-constructed based on public safety guidance and do not represent a comprehensive taxonomy of all possible real-world hazards. The benchmark lacks independent expert annotation.
