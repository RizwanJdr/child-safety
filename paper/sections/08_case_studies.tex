\subsection{Kitchen Burns}
A scenario involving a child reaching a hot stove. Interventions include stove guards or removing climbable furniture. The model explicitly tracks the \texttt{climbing} capability.

\subsection{Medicine Cabinet}
A scenario involving access to toxic substances. Interventions include high-mounted cabinets and child-resistant latches. The model tracks \texttt{reachObject} and \texttt{operateLatch}.

\subsection{Pool Access}
A scenario involving outdoor water hazards. Interventions include self-latching pool fences and door alarms. The formalisation captures the robustness required for multiple failure points.
